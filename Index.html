Project Idea

The project idea I have decided to go with is a mobile app that has the ability to pull sources from various different social feeds, the most popular being, Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, etc. Once all the sources have been pulled it would compile all the feeds simultaneously into one large news feed and messaging app. This app would be a ‘one-stop shop’ for an all in one experience which by design saves the user time when surfing their phone also reducing the distraction and hassle of switching between social apps.

According to online sources the average user spends 3 to 4 hours a day on their phone, whether its surfing social media or simply just for work purposes, heavy users spanning from 4 to 6 hours per day. This app would attempt to reduce the amount of time spent staring at your screen.

By having an app with all your social feeds in one place you could limit your time spent on your mobile each day and enjoy real-life experiences. Both IOS and Android have implemented a new app in their latest software update that monitors the time spent on your device. The app makes it possible to apply self-locking on specific apps that are being overused, this is possible due to the advanced monitoring system these two tech giants have implemented. It seems the latest trend in technology is to try to reduce the use of technology as in 2019 it plays such a big part in our lives. I believe a mobile app that has the ability to be an all in one replacement for the majority of other popular apps on your device is very powerful. Combined with IOS’ “Screen Time” and Google’s “Digital Wellbeing” it would be even easier to reduce screen on time. I can see this app being very appealing to a plethora of demographics, as versions of this app already exist and have received a fair amount of attention and downloads. I feel these apps that already exist don't do my product idea justice and can be produced and advertised in a much more polished manner. The goal I am trying to accomplish.

In order to go through with my idea, it will require me to learn a mobile app developing software, most popular programs being, Android Software Development, Swift and Flutter. I would have to do some research into each one of these programs and decide which one will best suit my needs and develop my app idea to its full potential. The tools ill need to run this software would be a Windows or Mac PC and an IOS or Android mobile device for software testing. I believe this is a very achievable product idea, As I have been interested in developing this mobile app long before I started studying ‘Information Technology’ at RMIT.
